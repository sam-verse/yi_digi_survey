How to Improve Your Scores:


General Safety:
To maximize digital safety, use strong passwords/PINs, biometric locks, limit social media use, stay updated on scams, always use HTTPS, restrict Bluetooth, and securely erase data before disposing devices. Be aware of official cybercrime reporting channels.
Password Management and Digital Identity Protection:
To maximize digital safety, use unique, complex passwords, change them frequently, leverage password managers, and enable two-factor authentication wherever available. Be highly protective of your Aadhaar number and only share it when absolutely necessary.
Social Media Security:
To maximize digital safety on social media, regularly review and update privacy settings, be cautious about accepting friend/connection requests from strangers, limit sharing of personal information, avoid location tagging, manage linked third-party apps, use strong passwords, access accounts only on trusted devices, and verify information before sharing.
Financial Digital Safety:
To maximize digital financial safety, regularly review online statements, use strong PINs/biometrics for payment apps, update account passwords frequently, be extremely cautious about sharing financial information, choose secure payment methods for e-commerce, and thoroughly verify website authenticity before providing sensitive data.
Phishing, Scams, and Cybercrime Awareness:
To maximize safety, be highly vigilant in identifying phishing attempts, never click on suspicious links or share sensitive information over unsolicited calls/messages, and always verify requests for money or personal data from known contacts. Proactively report any suspected scams to relevant authorities.
Mobile Device and App Security:
To maximize mobile security, only download apps from official stores, carefully review app permissions, use reputable antivirus/security software, avoid untrusted public WiFi connections, and promptly delete unused apps after clearing their data and permissions.
Cyberbullying, Online Harassment, and Child Online Protection:
To maximize safety, be aware of cyberbullying, harassment, and online exploitation, and know how to respond. If you experience or witness such issues, report them to the platforms, trusted authorities, and seek help. Educate yourself and others on recognizing and addressing these online threats.
Online Gaming and Entertainment Safety:
To maximize safety, always check age ratings before engaging with online content and games, communicate cautiously with strangers in gaming communities, be aware of potential cyberbullying, verify the legitimacy of gaming/entertainment platforms before use.
Digital Literacy, Fact-Checking, and Misinformation Awareness:
To maximize digital safety, be well-versed in identifying fake news and misinformation. Rely on reputable, fact-checking sources rather than social media shares or unverified claims when validating information. Exercise caution before spreading content that seems questionable or sensational.
