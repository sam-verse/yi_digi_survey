Masoom – Digital Safety Audit

Welcome to the Digital Safety Audit

The Digital Safety Audit helps you understand your current level of online security and identifies potential vulnerabilities that cybercriminals could exploit. This comprehensive evaluation reveals areas where you need additional safeguards and highlights the various ways your digital presence might be at risk.

This audit serves as your personal security assessment tool. By completing this evaluation, you'll gain a clear picture of your current security status and learn essential steps to strengthen your digital defense.

This is an initiative by Masoom, Young Indians
