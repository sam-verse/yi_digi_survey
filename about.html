Giving Voice To Young India

Young Indians (Yi) is a movement for Indian youth to Converge, Lead, Co-Create and influence India's Future. As an integral part of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), Yi's mission to strengthen the future of India gives a stage and voice to the country's next generation of changemakers. It aims to instill in young minds the power of leadership, enhance the youth entrepreneurial ecosystem and create youth-led changes to build the nation.
